About Us

Betaconnect Professional Consulting is a Nigerian based human capacity development firm established to deliver professional training and consulting to diverse public and private sectors in Nigeria and beyond. At Betaconnect, we work with individuals and corporate organization in many industries to ensure that their human resources personnel run in the most efficient way. We understand that every organization has a major need of developing her human resources for the success of the organization. This is achieved by organizing a wide variety of seminars and workshops that suits the needs of the organization.

Our Vision

To become the best and preferred platform for Human Capacity Development through Training and Consulting

Our Mission

To understand the Customer’s needs, solve its Problems and delight their customers.
