Job Title: Business Development Manager – Part Time

Location: Uyo, Akwa Ibom State and Abuja, FCT

Department: Marketing

Report to: MD/CEO

Position Summary

As a Business Development Manager your duty is to identify sales leads, manage business development activities for the organisation by driving sales activities and maintaining a good working relationship with new clients.

Major Responsibilities

Following up new business opportunities and setting up meetings

Planning and preparing presentations

Communicating new product developments to prospective clients

Overseeing the development of marketing literature

Writing reports

Providing the MD/CEO with feedback.

Application Closing Date

27th June, 2016

Education:

This position requires bachelor’s degree or HND from fresh graduates in any field of studies. 2–3 years of sales or marketing experience is an added advantage.

How to Apply
