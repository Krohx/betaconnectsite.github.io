Livespeech

Presentation Skills Training in Lagos

A 2-Day workshop to learn how to:

Articulate stronger and more concise presentations

Convey your ideas & messages with clarity and simplicity

Cut down the time it takes to plan and prepare a presentation

Deliver your presentation with energy, purpose and impact

Overcome public speaking anxiety and stage fright

This is a practical training. Participants will make individual presentations before the group, which will be evaluated & critiqued on video playback.

 

Please come along with a laptop computer for content development and presentation drills. Dress code is formal or corporate. Class size is limited. Book your seat early.

WHO SHOULD ATTEND?

Anyone who has to speak or make presentations as duty demands. Your audience might be colleagues, clients, investors or in-house Management executives. Previous training participants include engineers, bankers, oil & gas professionals, real estate developers, network marketers, lawyers, public health workers, medical doctors, University lecturers, business analysts, ICT professionals, authors, media professionals and more. 

 

If you want to learn how to speak in public and deliver more effective PowerPoint presentations, the LiveSpeech Open Workshop is a great place to start. Corporate organizations and institutions are welcome to register members and staff for this training.
